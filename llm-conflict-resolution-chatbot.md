# Proactive Conflict Resolution in LLM-Powered Chatbot

## 🧠 Context
A global crypto exchange platform with 5M+ users used an LLM chatbot for support.

## ⚠️ Problem
- Late escalation detection
- Public complaints
- High agent workload

## 🔍 Why It Matters
- Impacts trust and retention
- Increases operational costs

## 🧪 Approach
- Multi-model LLM chatbot
- Predictive escalation classifier
- Reinforcement learning optimization

## 🛠️ Product Implementation
- Multi-channel chatbot integration
- Human-in-loop system

## ⚖️ Trade-offs
- Automation vs empathy
- Latency vs quality

## 🌍 Ethical Lens
- Emotional manipulation risks
- Transparency in AI usage

## 💡 PM Takeaways
- Timing of intervention is critical
- AI should augment humans

## 📊 Outcomes
- 40% reduction in disputes
- 21% increase in satisfaction

## 📦 Datasets
- Chat transcripts
- Product data
