# Predictive Supplier Performance in Travel Platform

## 🧠 Context
A global travel platform needed better supplier evaluation.

## ⚠️ Problem
- Reactive evaluation
- Booking failures
- Price instability

## 🔍 Why It Matters
- Impacts customer experience
- Reduces trust

## 🧪 Approach
- Time-series forecasting
- Classification and regression models
- MCDM scoring (AHP, TOPSIS)

## 🛠️ Product Implementation
- Ranking dashboards
- Actionable insights

## ⚖️ Trade-offs
- Complexity vs explainability

## 🌍 Ethical Lens
- Fairness in supplier ranking

## 💡 PM Takeaways
- Multi-dimensional evaluation is key

## 📊 Outcomes
- 40% reduction in disruptions
- 20% improvement in ROI

## 📦 Datasets
- Booking data
- Pricing data
- Reviews
